Auditing Services

Security Auditing

WizSec's goal is to hire the best security experts in the industry from all over the world. Our team have many years of experience of best security practises both in bitcoin and regular financial services. We offer a full rage of auditing services, from code assessment to penetration testing to help you protect your bitcoin assets in the best possible way. When you get an audit from WizSec we will give you a full disclosure report of all the findings packaged in an encrypted document. We will also help you set up an action plan on proposed changes after the audit.

Performing security audits on a regular basis will not only make you sleep better at night, but it also makes your customers feel safe. That way you business can attract more customers since security is a big concern when choosing a bitcoin service. The bumpy history of bitcoin makes customers very weary of security and will often think twice before depositing funds.
